The Restaurant

Located in Tamil nadu, chennai, Leo's Das Esszimmer is a cozy, family-run restaurant offering Italian, Mediterranean, and Southern Italian cuisine. Managed by an Italian chef and his partner, the restaurant is praised for its warm hospitality and authentic dishes like spaghetti al scampi and grilled fish. It's a favorite among locals and tourists alike, known for its intimate atmosphere and exceptional service.
