Baked Potato Pasta is a comforting and hearty dish that blends two carb-rich favorites: pasta and potatoes. Salted Fried Chicken

₹450

Salted Fried Chicken is a savory and flavorful dish that involves marinating chicken in a salt-based mixture, then deep-frying it to achieve a crispy, golden crust while keeping the meat juicy inside. It is enjoyed in various forms across different cuisines, especially in Asian, Southern American, and Caribbean cooking.

Italian Mushroom Sauce is a rich, savory sauce made primarily with mushrooms and often combined with garlic, onions, olive oil or butter, and Italian herbs. It's a versatile condiment used in Italian cuisine to enhance pasta, risotto, meats, and even polenta..
